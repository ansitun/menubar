Hello this plugin is a sticky top menubar, which you can add to your site.

You just need to put the menus and submenus in form of "ul" and "li" and put it in a "div" tag.

Then you must pass the id of the div tag to the "menubar()" function.

I have added dummy content to the "li" of the menubar. You can add your important links in the menubar.
